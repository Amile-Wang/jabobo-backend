
> **GN Communication Equipment (China) Co., Ltd.** (hereinafter referred to as *GN China*) is a wholly foreign-owned enterprise established in 2001 in the Xiamen Torch High-Tech Industrial Development Zone. The company is a subsidiary of **GN Store Nord A/S**, whose headquarters are located in Copenhagen, Denmark.
>
> GN Store Nord A/S was founded in 1869 and, in 1871, established the *Great Northern Telegraph* on Gulangyu Island in Xiamen, connecting communications between Europe and Asia.
>
> For many years, the GN Group has been committed to providing communication solutions for customers worldwide. Its brand portfolio includes **Jabra**, **ReSound**, **SteelSeries**, **BlueParrot**, **Falcom**, and others.
>
> Its product lines cover call-center headsets, Bluetooth communication devices, hearing aids, mobile music products, video conferencing integrations, and gaming mice and keyboards, making the group a global leader in the headset industry.
>
> The group’s flagship brand, **Jabra**, enjoys strong brand recognition and an excellent reputation, and has repeatedly won international industrial design awards and “most popular product” honors. Supported by extensive experience in wireless communication and audio technologies, Jabra’s products are no longer limited to the consumer market; they also offer professional solutions for various industries, meeting the needs of medical-grade, professional-grade, and consumer-grade markets.
>
> GN China has unique expertise and research capabilities in areas including industrial design, numerical simulation, electroacoustic design, intelligent sensor technology, touch technology, active noise cancellation, and auditory comfort design.
