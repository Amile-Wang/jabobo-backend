Introduction to Simulation Team 
Simulation Team is a shared function under the OneGN roof, located in Xiamen.
Responsible for simulation driven design and validation across different domains, including: mechanical, thermal, optical, vibro-acoustic, computational fluid dynamics...and always be passionate looking into new category of simulations to solve issues we face in product development.
Their mission is to drive high-quality product design through simulation, reducing development costs and time while improving product performance and reliability.
To know more about them, you could visit their homepage on my GN website.

Core Capabilities for Simulation Team
Simulation team's expertise spans a wide range of domains:
Structural Analysis: Drop tests, strength checks, headband evaluations.
Thermal Simulation: Natural convection, passive/fan cooling solutions.
Optical Simulation: Ray tracing, laser welding, LED luminance analysis.
Electromagnetic & CFD: Wireless charging, airflow, droplet movement.
Vibro-acoustic Analysis: Resonance frequency, driver basket behavior.
Material Validation: Sustainable plastics, naming rules, supplier templates.

They also manage a Material Database and use tools like COMSOL, Fluent, Altair, and Abaqus across multiple workstations.
