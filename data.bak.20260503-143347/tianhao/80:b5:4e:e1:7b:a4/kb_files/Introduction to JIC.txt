Brief intro
JIC is short for "Jabra innovation Club", a dynamic initiative that has been thriving at our 厦门 site since its inception. it was created to foster a culture of innovation among our colleagues. 
Founders
It was founded by Susie, with the support and approval of LiLi, and now Susie and Daphi are Principles of JIC.

Core goals
Enhancing the innovation culture;
Inspiring our people;
Streamlining the innovation process.

Slogan
Innovation with fun

Activities
The activities have been organized by volunteers from cross functional teams.
including lectures, sharing sessions, workshops, competitions, and awards,
providing a platform for colleagues to learn, gain inspiration, and collaborate across functions to develop their ideas.
